Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I enroll in a course?

To enroll in a course, simply browse the available courses, click on the "Enroll Now" button, and follow the on-screen instructions to complete your enrollment.

Can I track my course progress?

Yes! You can track your progress in each course from your dashboard, where you'll see your completion percentage for each module.

What should I do if I forget my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll be guided through the steps to reset it via your email address.

How to Use SuperEdu

Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Create an account or log in to your existing account.

  3. Enroll in your preferred course by clicking the "Enroll Now" button.
  4. Track your progress from your personal dashboard.
  5. If you need help, use the resources provided in this Help Center.
